Simple CS:GO Sticker Search

Search the steam market for CSGO skins with stickers!

Searching for skins with stickers on the CS:GO community market can be downright frustrating. This tool helps you search for skins with rare stickers (e.g. Katowice 2014, Crown Foil or Howling Dawn) on the steam market - without the hassle of looking up exact sticker names and fiddling with the less-than-optimal search options on the csgo market.

frequently asked questions

How does this work?

This tool generates search URLs for the steam market. When you click "search", the csgo market gets opened in a new tab and displays the results.

How do I use this?

Add stickers by clicking them or dragging them onto one of the four slots. Select which wear states should be included in the search results, then click the search button.

What is the difference between Search and Search (ignore order)?

Search searches for stickers in the specified order. If you search for A-B-A-C, only skins that have the stickers in that exact order will be found.

Search (ignore order) searches for skins that have the stickers, regardless of order.
Each sticker will only be considered once. If you search for A-A-B-A the steam market will return skins that have A and B at least once.
